Privacy Policy for ProximaLink

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

1. Introduction

ProximaLink ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our automation services and integrations with platforms like Pinterest, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram.

2. Data We Collect

We access the following data via social platform APIs solely to provide automation services:

  • Profile Data: To identify your account for posting (e.g., Name, ID).
  • Board & Page Lists: To allow you to select where to post content.
  • Analytics: To display performance metrics (clicks, views) in your dashboard.
  • Access Tokens: Encrypted tokens required to authenticate with platforms on your behalf.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To publish content (Pins, Posts) that you schedule.
  • To generate performance reports for your internal use.
  • To maintain the connection between your account and our services.

4. Data Retention & Revocation

We only retain access tokens and data as long as you have an active account with ProximaLink. You can revoke our access at any time via the Security Settings of the respective social platform (e.g., Pinterest Settings > Apps).

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All communication occurs via encrypted HTTPS connections, and access tokens are stored in secure, encrypted databases.
